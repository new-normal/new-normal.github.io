    Stay Protected in Quarantine

  • Socially distance six feet from others.
  • Wash hands often and well for 20 seconds.
  • Use soap and water or an alcohol-based hand sanitizer.
  • Avoid touching your face with unwashed hands.
  • Cough or sneeze into the inside of your elbow.
  • Keep your distance from anyone who has cold or flu-like symptoms.
  • Contact your health care provider if you have a fever, cough or difficulty breathing.
  • Limit trips to stores for essentials only.
  • Disinfect frequently touched hard surfaces (doorknobs, counters, etc.) using bleach (1/3 cup per gallon of water), a 70% alcohol solution, or other disinfectants recommended by the EPA.
